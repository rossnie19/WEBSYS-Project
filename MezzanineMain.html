Our Brand

Timeless. Cultural. Authentic.

We are a restaurant that brings together Filipino and French cuisine in a way that feels natural and intentional. Our food is inspired by Filipino flavors that many of us grew up with, paired with the structure, techniques, and attention to detail found in French cooking. The result is a menu that respects both cultures while creating something distinct.

Our approach to cooking is rooted in care and discipline. We focus on quality ingredients, thoughtful preparation, and balanced flavors. Filipino cuisine gives our dishes warmth and familiarity, while French techniques allow us to refine textures, presentation, and depth. Each plate is designed to be approachable, well-crafted, and satisfying.

We value heritage and craftsmanship in everything we do. Our team is dedicated to honoring the rich culinary traditions of both cultures while pushing boundaries to create a dining experience that is uniquely Mezzanine.

We cook to bring people together and to create a place where guests feel welcome and cared for.

Our Origins

"We wanted to build a place where a Parisian chef would feel at home eating Kare-Kare."

What began as a conversation over coffee in Le Marais became a mission in Manila. Founders Chef Alain and Isabella realized that Filipino and French cultures share a core value: the meal is sacred. Mezzanine was born from the desire to elevate this shared ritual.
