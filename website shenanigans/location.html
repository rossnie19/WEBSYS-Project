Getting Here

By Private Vehicle

Ample parking is available at Active Fun Building. Enter through the main entrance on 9th Avenue. Valet parking service is available during peak hours.

By Public Transport

Take the BGC Bus and alight at Market! Market! or High Street stops. From there, it's a short 5-10 minute walk to our location. Grab and taxi services are also readily available in BGC.
