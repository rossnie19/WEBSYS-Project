Our Brand

Timeless. Elegant. Authentic.

Mezzanine bridges the gap between the ancestral homes of the Philippines and the grand brasseries of Paris. We do not just serve food; we serve memories refined by technique. Our commitment is to the boldness of Filipino flavors and the discipline of French culinary art.

How It All Began

"We wanted to build a place where a Parisian chef would feel at home eating Kare-Kare."

What began as a conversation over coffee in Le Marais became a mission in Manila. Founders Chef Alain and Isabella realized that Filipino and French cultures share a core value: the meal is sacred. Mezzanine was born from the desire to elevate this shared ritual.
